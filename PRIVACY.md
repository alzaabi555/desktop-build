Privacy Policy for "Rased" App

1. Introduction
Welcome to "Rased" (راصد). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information and your students' data remain secure. 

2. Data Collection and Usage
"Rased" is a standalone, offline desktop application. We DO NOT collect, track, transmit, or store any personal data, student information, grades, or usage analytics on any external servers. 

3. Data Storage
All data entered into the application (such as student names, attendance records, and grades) is stored locally and securely on your personal computer's hard drive. You have full control over your data.

4. Third-Party Access
Since the app operates completely offline, no third-party services, advertisers, or organizations have access to your data.

5. App Permissions
The app only requires standard local storage permissions to save your database and export reports (such as PDF or Excel files) directly to your device.
